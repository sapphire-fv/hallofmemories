History



Welcome to the history of Sapphire S.Y. 2011-2021. We have made a lot of history from the past and this section will bring you nostalgic vibes and make us go back in memory lane.



--- Primary Days ---



Grade 1-C


Welcome to the start of the Memories in Sapphire. Welcome back to the simple life of Grade 1 wherein we had fun activities such as Primary Fun Day, Field Trip and Camping. They're probably the memories that you do not remember, but these were the times wherein we had the most fun in our stay in O.B. Fairview. O.B. just progressingly keeps getting worse in their management. It became obvious after Grade 1. We had only one teacher for all major subjects which was our advisor and we only meet new teachers during special subjects such as our OG teacher Sir Cases. Also, this is the lowest number of Gems in Sapphire since we were only 18 in the class.
